Upcoming Nations League Games

Portugal vs France - 11/14/2020

Defending EURO champions Portugal goes head to head with the defending World Cup champions France in Lisbon's Estadio da Luz. France will be looking for redemption as Portugal previously beat them in the EURO finals. Both teams have super star lineups with Cristiano Ronaldo and the wonderkid Joao Felix most likely to be spearheading the attack. On the other side, Antoine Griezmann and another young star, Kylian Mbappe will be taking point. This is certainly a must watch fixture for the weekend.

Germany vs Ukraine - 11/14/2020

Germany takes on Ukraine in Leipzig this weekend. Typically this would be a relatively routine win for Germany; however, Ukraine are coming off of a big win against Spain and are hoping to carry that momentum into this fixture. This is an important game for Germany as they are 1 point behind group leaders Spain.

San Marino vs Gibraltar - 11/14/2020

San Marino kicks off against Gibraltar in the Stadio Olimpico di Serravalle. Gibraltar are absolutely running riot in the Nation's League D group 2, with two 1-0 victories in their first two matches. This is no suprise as Gibraltar have been on fire as of late.
