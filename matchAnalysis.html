Liverpool vs Manchester City
Post-Match Analysis

This was the big fixture of the last weekend. Current title holders Liverpool taking on Pep Guardiola's Man City is always a tight match. The game started rapidly in the 13th minute with a penalty goal from Salah after a poor challenge from Kyle Walker on Mane. Gabriel Jesus then went on to score for Man City in the 31st minute to tie the game at 1-1. Then Man City were handed a golden chance to take the lead when Kevin de Bruyne stepped up to take a penalty kick, but the ever so reliable midfielder suprisingly missed his mark. From then on, each team had a few chances, but neither were able to capatalize. All in all, not a bad result for Liverpool who are creeping back to the top spot in the table; however, a win was much needed for City as they are struggling to gain footing in the league.
