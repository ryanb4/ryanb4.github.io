OVERVIEW

The StackSet data structure utilizes characteristics of both a stack and a set. Similarly to a set, a StackSet will contain unique elements. It will also behave much as a stack, using a "last in first out" order. This implementation uses a singly linked list of nodes.

The StackSet uses methods typical of a stack such as push, pop, and peek/top, while maintaing the uniqueness of a set. Futhermore, the size of the StackSet does not exceed the limit established by its constructor.
